About Us

We write.
We report.
We roar.

The Lion's Pride is the official English newspaper of Joongdong High School (중동고등학교). Founded by students, for students — we cover campus news, global affairs, culture, and opinion, all in English.

Our mission is to sharpen our voices and broaden our perspectives. Every issue is crafted with careful journalism, thoughtful editing, and genuine curiosity about the world around us.
